QUESTIONS

What is the address of the wedding?

The Bluegrass Barn
144 S Main St. Crittenden, KY 41030

What is the time of the wedding?

The ceremony begins at 4:00 PM, followed by social hour at 5:00 PM and the reception at 6:00 PM.

What should I wear?

We expect a semi-formal attire so you can stay comfortable and not worry about meeting high expectations.

Should I bring a gift?

Gifts are never required but always appreciated. Our webpage has a gifts section including a link to our Amazon registry where gifts can be sent straight to us, or feel free to bring a gift straight to the wedding.

Are plus-ones allowed?

We attempted to offer plus one's to everyone who requires them. Please contact the bride to inquire about additional plus ones.

Are children allowed?

The wedding party would prefer to host this event for our grown-up friends and family. We do understand the difficulties in honoring this request, so please contact the bride if you have unavoidable circumstances.

Where can I park?

Our venue will provide parking, so travel directly to the listed address.
